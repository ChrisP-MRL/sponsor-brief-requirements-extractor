### Objectives

1. Phase 1 of project is not to conduct a detailed draft survey of OGV.
2. Phase 1 of project is to test the practicality of using drones to conduct draft survey
3. Phase 1 is determine a safe launch/landing location for offshore drone operations
4. Phase 1 is to utilise existing MinRes drone equipment
5. Phase 1 is to utilise internal MinRes expertise in marine operations, drone piloting, surveying, and technology.
6. Phase 1 is to ensure proper understanding of CASA laws and obligations in respect 
7. Phase 1 is not to interfere with offshore loading operations.
8. Phase 1 is ensure operational safety and relevant MinRes processes are observed.
